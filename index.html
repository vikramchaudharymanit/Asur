CHITRA


CHITRA is ASUR Robotics’ flagship quadruped robot designed for real-world surveillance, terrain adaptability, and intelligent autonomy. With its four-legged design, CHITRA navigates environments that wheels can’t—making it ideal for campus patrolling, industrial inspection, and smart security systems. Built to be both affordable and advanced, CHITRA is a fusion of precision engineering, AI vision, and responsive mobility. It’s more than just a machine—it’s the future of intelligent security on the move.

    About Us

    We are ASUR Robotics, a student-led robotics research and innovation team.

    ASUR Robotics is the official robotics startup of NIT Bhopal, committed to building India’s most advanced and affordable security robots. At the heart of our innovation is CHITRA—a cutting-edge quadruped robot designed for real-world surveillance, agility, and intelligent response. Backed by prestigious recognition and funding at E-Summit, ASUR stands at the intersection of innovation and impact, driven by a team passionate about reshaping the future of security and automation.
